This page shows an assessment and rubric that I made for a 9th grade high school class about different ways to factor. This demonstrates the work that I put into my assessment to make sure they give me as much data as possible to help me make informed decisions when teaching in the classroom. Using effective and frequent assessments (formal and informal), I am better able to determine where students are confused so I can address those problems. The rubrics I make help me effectively determine and communicate how students are doing with the material.
