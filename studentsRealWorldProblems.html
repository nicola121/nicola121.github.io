One summer, I was a YMCA STEM teacher. One of my favorite activities that we did was teaching them how to order their favorite meals at a variety of resteraunts. I was surprised by how many students didn't know what a combo meal was. We then led the rest of the activity where we helped them learn more about fractions and dividing costs evenly amongst friends. This helped the students see why math is actually important in their lives. This was an activity made for younger students, but the principles of creating engaging lesson plans still applies to older kids. I try to make my classroom activities enjoyable and relatable so they will help the students for the rest of their lives.
